Do I need a VPN to use Sportzfy?

Most cricket and football streams work without one. A handful of channels are region-locked by the upstream source. For those, a VPN can help. We don't bundle one or recommend a specific paid VPN.

What sports can I watch on Sportzfy TV?

Cricket (IPL, PSL, BPL, T20 and ODI World Cups, Asia Cup, county cricket), football (Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, Champions League, FIFA World Cup, AFC Asian Cup), MotoGP, boxing, UFC, and a set of regional sports channels.
